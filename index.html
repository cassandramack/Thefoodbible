Feast For A Summer Day

A meal for groups of friends on a hot summer day!

Homemade Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 tbsp sugar
  • 3 tbsp butter (melted)
  • 3 tbsp butter (room temperature)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combined all of the dry ingredients together.
  2. Add in the egg, melted butter, milk, oil and vanilla to the dry ingredients.
  3. Heat the stove to medium heat.
  4. Add 1 tbsp of butter to the pan and allow to melt.
  5. In the pan, pour batter to create 4 pancakes in the pan and allow to cook until bubbles begin to form on the top.
  6. Once bubbles have begun to form, flip the pancakes and leave for another minute or until fully cooked.
  7. Remove from pan and set aside. Repeat steps 4-6 until all pancake batter has been cooked.
  8. Serve immedicately with maple syrup and berrries, and enjoy!
